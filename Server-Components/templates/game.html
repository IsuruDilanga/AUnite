Let's Play Games

Memory Card Game

The cards have a pictures on one side and each picture appears on two cards. The game starts with all the cards face down and players take turns to turn over two cards.

Introduction

  • The game starts with all the cards face down and players take turns to turn over two cards
  • If the two cards have the same picture, then they keep the cards, otherwise they turn the cards face down again.
  • Play continues until all the cards have been mached
  • You can Choose the level according to your child

Tic - Tac - Toe

Two players take turns in drawing either an ` O' or an ` X' in one square of a grid consisting of nine squares. The winner is the first player to get three of the same symbols in a row.

Introduction

  • The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares
  • You are X, your child is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  • The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  • When all 9 squares are full, the game is over

Snake Game

The player controls a long, thin creature, resembling a snake, which roams around on a bordered plane, picking up food

Introduction

  • The player has to uses the arrow keys to move a "snake" around the board
  • As the snake finds food, it eats the food, and thereby grows larger.
  • The game ends when the snake either moves off the screen or moves into itself
  • The goal is to make the snake as large as possible before that happens

Flying Plane

The player can control the plane, The plane should move by arrow keys for not getting hit by other objects.

Introduction

  • The player has to uses the arrow keys to move the plane around the board
  • Move plane to avoid getting hit by other objects
  • The goal is try to avoid getting hit by other other object as much as possible
